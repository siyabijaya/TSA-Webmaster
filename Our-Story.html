OUR STORY
Bombay Bliss was inspired from the childhoods of co-founders Bijaya and Nadgonde who were best friends living in the bustling city of Bombay. Their experiences in the streets of their homeland led to the idea of creating a sustainable and eco-friendly restaurant together in order to raise awareness to protecting the environment. Together, they share their heritage through cooking vegan/vegetarian Indian cuisine that nourishes the mind, body, and soul. By partering with local organic farms, the best friends make the first step into a more sustainable and healthy world.
Masala chai is a traditional tea that is drank throughout the day. Its aroma comes from the organic spices native to India, and it is most enjoyed on a cold day.
Chef Bijaya's passion for cooking comes from growing up in a vibrant Indian community along with his best friend Nadgonde. Ever since he was little kid his heart and mind always went towards the betterment of the nature around, so he did just that with his cooking.
The Bombay Breakfast Bowl is a specialty of the restaurant with produce from local farms and spices freshly picked from the forests in India. The freshness of the bowl is sure to wake you up.
Chef Nadgonde's inspiration for the creation of Bombay Bliss came from his own family and the memories he made as a child along with Chef Bijaya. His culinary expertise with Indian flavors comes from studying techniques and uses for specifc spices. His skills make the dishes step to the next level.
The South Indian Breakfast platter is grand and provides anybody with a taste of home. The traditional flavors and fresh ingredients can reach your soul with just a few bites.
